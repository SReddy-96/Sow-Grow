🌱 What is Sow & Grow? 🌱
Sow & Grow is the gardener's best friend. Easily add your plants and key details, then let the app create a personalized year planner. See at a glance when to sow, water, and care for each plant throughout the seasons. No more guesswork - Sow & Grow keeps your green thumb on track.

How it all works?

Add a Plant

First, you'll want to add a plant, inputting data like sowing months, light requirements, disease control, and much more. Once submitted to the database, this information becomes accessible for the next step.

Add a Year

Next, you'll want to create a new year. This is where the magic happens. Here, you can add your plants for the year, and the app will display all the relevant data in an easy-to-read, calendar-style format.

Add a Review

Lastly, as you sow, transfer, and harvest your plants, you can input the corresponding dates to keep track of the year's growth cycle. This allows you to review each plant individually, making it easier to plan and improve your gardening efforts year after year.
